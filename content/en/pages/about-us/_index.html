Degrowth  is organised as   a free, open-access, international, transdisciplinary, and peer-reviewed journal that focuses on advancing the goals of degrowth. It is published online in annual volumes, with rolling open submission set to be begin in early 2024. Organised as an international, independent, non-profit association, the editorial collective is decentralised across various countries. We meet regularly and operate following the principles of radical democracy. We are currently running without funding or any editorial affiliation, but we are supported by the University of Glasgow through Glasgow Open Journals , who host Degrowth  submissions on the SCURL Open Journal Systems (OJS) platform.
